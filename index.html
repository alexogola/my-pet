My pet dog

Many people keep dogs as pets. I too have a pet dog. My dog belongs to Pomerian breed.
it is white in colour. I call it Max. His body is covered with soft, white fur.
Max is small, active, smart and runs very fast.

I love my dog

I love my pet dog. I take great care of it and give him bath.
Every morning I take him out for walk. Thus I too am able to have a walk every morning. I feel my dog personally.
I give him biscuits, bread and milk and an egg every day, and twice a week I give him meat.
